Site Purpose

The main purpose of this site will be a place that the end user can come and get basic weather data. The consumable will be a subscription to enhanced features as the site progresses. Site progression will be a two-part process. First will be basic designs that are created and collaborated by the team. Second will come from end-user suggestions. Feedback will be reviewed monthly and implemented based on amount of requests and cost of build out.
